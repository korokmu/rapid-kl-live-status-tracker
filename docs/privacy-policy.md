# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 8 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Rapid KL Live Status Tracker ("the Service", "we") collects, uses, and protects your personal data. This policy is written in compliance with Malaysia's **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**.

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

### Data you provide
| Data | When | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Email address | When you register an account | To authenticate your login |
| Marketing consent flag | At signup (optional opt-in) | To send updates about this and related future projects, only if you agree |

### Data generated by your use
| Data | When | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Train status reports | When you submit a report | To power the live status display |
| Comments | When you post a comment | To share ground-truth information with other commuters |
| Upvotes | When you confirm a report | To verify accuracy of reports |
| Account creation timestamp | At signup | For record-keeping |

---

## 2. What We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your real name
- We do not collect your phone number
- We do not collect your location
- We do not track you across other websites

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- **Email address**: Used only for login authentication. If you opted in, also used to send updates about this service and related future projects from the same developer. We retain your email for this purpose until you delete your account or withdraw consent.
- **Reports, comments, upvotes**: Displayed publicly on the site to help other commuters. Exported as anonymised CSV (no emails, no names) for statistical analysis.

---

## 4. How Long We Keep Your Data

| Data | Retention |
|---|---|
| Account (email, consent flag) | Until you delete your account |
| Reports, comments, upvotes | Deleted from live database nightly (after export) |
| Exported CSVs (anonymised) | Kept indefinitely for statistical analysis — contain no personal data |

---

## 5. Who Can See Your Data

- **Your reports and comments** are visible to all visitors of the site (no login required to view)
- **Your email address** is never shown publicly and is never sold or shared with third parties
- **Anonymised activity data** (no name, no email) may be included in public CSV exports

---

## 6. Your Rights (Under PDPA 2010)

You have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate personal data
- **Delete** your account and personal data
- **Withdraw consent** for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

---

## 7. Data Security

- All data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL), protected by Row Level Security (RLS)
- Passwords are never stored — login is handled by Supabase Auth
- Your email is never exported to any external storage, public repository, or third party

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We will update this page if our data practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.

---

## 9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or your data, please open an issue on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com) or contact the developer directly.
